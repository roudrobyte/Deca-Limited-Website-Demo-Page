About Deca Limited

From Warriors to Industry Leaders

Deca Limited (formerly known as Deca Warriors) is a sports tournament management organization based in Kishoreganj, Bangladesh. Founded in 2024 as a competitive cricket team, the organization subsequently transitioned into sports event management, tournament planning, and athlete development. The organization was founded by Sajidul Islam Roudro under its original name, Deca Warriors, utilizing the tagline "An unstoppable being!".

📜 History

Deca Warriors was established in 2024 with the primary objective of building a competitive regional cricket team while fostering local athletic talent and sportsmanship. The team gained initial prominence through its structured operational management and participation in regional cricket tournaments. Following its foundational success, the organization underwent a strategic rebranding, changing its name to Deca Limited. This transition reflected a broader institutional vision to expand beyond cricket operations into comprehensive sports event management, tournament logistics, and youth sports promotion.

🏆 Achievements

Shortly after its inception, Deca Warriors competed in the Arafat Smriti Sangshad Mini Cricket Tournament. The team concluded the tournament as the 2nd Runners-up, establishing its early competitive reputation in local cricket circuits.
